Sam Garfield

My name is Sam Garfield. I live in Boston pursuing a Computer Science degree at Tufts.

In my spare time, I like being active. I run on the cross country and track teams at Tufts, I cycle, and I hike, but anything outdoors is awesome. This past summer I joined Bike & Build, a non-profit that raises money and awareness for affordable housing. Joining 32 other riders, I spent 82 days cycling from Charleston, South Carolina to Santa Cruz, California, totalling over 4200 miles! It was by far the best adventure and summer experience I've ever had. If this interests you, check out Bike & Build's website!

I'm a fan of Mustachianism and Minimalism. I'm also a big supporter of the Acorn Head.
