Sam Garfield

Hi! I'm Sam Garfield. I'm from the great state of Pennsylvania, and I lived in Boston while pursuing a Computer Science degree at Tufts University.

In my spare time, I like being active. I was a runner on the cross country and track teams at Tufts, and now I cycle, rock climb, hike, but anything outdoors is awesome. Last summer I joined Bike & Build, a non-profit that raises money and awareness for affordable housing. Joining 32 other riders, I spent 82 days cycling from Charleston, South Carolina to Santa Cruz, California, totalling over 4200 miles! It was by far the best adventure and summer experience I've ever had. If this interests you, check out Bike & Build's website!

This is my favorite animal.

I'm a fan of Mustachianism and Minimalism. I'm also a big supporter of the Acorn Head, located on the Tufts campus.
